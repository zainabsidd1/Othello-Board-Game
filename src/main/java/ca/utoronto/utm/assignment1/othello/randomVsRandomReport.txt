We will use math to calculate the p-value of our results.
Probability P1 wins = p^ =0.4551
P0 (assumed under H0) = 0.5
Z = (p^-p0)/(sqrt(p0(1-p0))/n)
Z = (0.4551-0.5)/(sqrt(0.5(1-0.5))/10000)
Z = -8.98
This yields a p-value of p<0.00001. This means our observed difference is unlikely, and so we reject H0 and accept Ha.
